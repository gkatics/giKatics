The only iOS app that focuses exclusively on the dangerous impact of noise pollution on health and mental wellbeing

The app was developed by a group of master class students of the Apple Developer Academy in Napoli, Italy, and is available for free on the Apple App Store as first app published in the 2017/2018 academy alumni. Users can measure and calculate the actual noise level in their current position and obtain further analysis to make better decisions on how to avoid high noise levels over time. Simultaneously, all users contribute as "citizen scientists" to the creation of a global map of noise level distribution.
