UX and UI for BeMySpace

BeMySpace is an app that aims to match rehearsal spaces with performing artists everywhere. In the app, the user can book a place with the structure and details that the user needs to set free their dance routine.
The project was to develop the UX and UI for the new product. Was a complex project as it mixes host and guest profiles. The app should had an easy navigation and an UI that pleased our persona. The logo was conceptualized to bring dance in a shaped place.

april, 2019

Create generative (he)artworks

heart/work is the only iOS app that generates original artwork based on your unique health information. Bridging the gap between wellbeing and health data, heart/work creates layered visuals to provide at-a-glance synopsis using meditative breathing exercises, your heartbeat, health data from the Apple Watch and environmental data from your current surroundings.
The project were developed in 3 months in a team formed by Marco Falanga, Ottavio Gelone, Baldev Ghelani, Giselle Katics, Mikey Krieger and Moritz Philip Recke. heart/work was the graduation project in the Apple Developer Academy in Napoli, Italy.

Redesign for DanceMe+

DanceMe+ is an iOS app that supports and encourage dance creation. In the app, dancers can create their own studio, uploading videos and sharing experiences about dance.
The project was to updated the existing design of the old app. Consistency was the mindset and the goal was to modernise the app to create a better experience in the current devices. New features was also added, while keeping the UI consistent to the DanceMe brand. The user experience was heavily restructured to bring more pleasure to its use.

april, 2018

Playground Book for the WWDC18 submission

Memory is the "mental capacity of retaining and reviving facts, events, impressions, and recalling previous experiences". But how we create a memory and why we record some events and forget others?
The Playground Book try to unriddle why and how we record a particular event using illustration, animation and a personal narrative. The book has 5 chapters and pursue a path to explain how a moment became a memory and are stored in our brain.
The Playground was developed as part of application to the WWDC18 scholarship program. Coding and drawings were made by me.
Scholarship winner.

NoiseGate is the only iOS app that focuses exclusively on the dangerous impact of noise pollution on health and mental wellbeing

The app was developed by a group of master class students of the Apple Developer Academy in Napoli, Italy, and is available for free on the Apple App Store as first app published in the 2017/2018 academy alumni. Users can measure and calculate the actual noise level in their current position and obtain further analysis to make better decisions on how to avoid high noise levels over time. Simultaneously, all users contribute as "citizen scientists" to the creation of a global map of noise level distribution.
